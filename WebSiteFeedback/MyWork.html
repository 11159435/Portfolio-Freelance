My Freelance Experience – 3D Designer

My experience as a freelance 3D designer was very valuable and exciting. I worked with various clients on a wide range of tasks: from creating models for games to visualizing interiors and advertising projects.

One of the biggest advantages of this experience was constant communication with people from all over the world. I learned to express my ideas clearly, listen to clients’ wishes, and explain complex technical points in simple terms. This helped me improve my negotiation skills, resolve conflicts, and reach compromises.

While working on projects, I mastered many new tools and technologies: I improved my knowledge of Blender, 3ds Max, and other 3D modeling packages, and also learned to quickly adapt to new requirements or changes in a project. I became more organized, learned to plan my time, set realistic deadlines, and stick to them responsibly.

What skills did I gain?
• Professional 3D modeling and visualization
• Ability to work with different programs and plugins
• Effective communication with clients
• Organization of my own work and time management
• Solving non-standard tasks and creative approach
• Working in a multitasking environment

This freelance experience taught me to be flexible, responsible, and always open to new knowledge. I am confident that the experience I have gained will help me in future professional achievements and development.
